Flight Planning Tools

Performance calculators and OFP based on POH data

Operational Flight Plan
Navigation log, wind triangle, fuel planning (VFR/IFR), mass & balance with CG envelope. Save, load, and print.
Climb / Cruise / Descent
Time, distance, and fuel for climb and descent. Cruise TAS, RPM, fuel flow, and range by altitude, temperature, and power setting.
Takeoff & Landing
Ground roll and 50 ft obstacle clearance distances. Accounts for OAT, pressure altitude, weight, wind, flaps, and surface conditions.
